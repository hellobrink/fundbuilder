# Open Innovation Fund Builder

A professional method card application for building effective grant programmes. This interactive tool helps users select stages, explore method cards, and build their funding strategy.

## Features

- **7 Grant Building Stages**: From Design & Thesis Development to Portfolio Management
- **Interactive Method Cards**: Flip cards to reveal detailed guidance and advice
- **Banking System**: Save important cards to build your comprehensive strategy
- **Export Functionality**: Download your selected cards as text files
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Clean, Modern UI**: Inspired by minimal design principles

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Select a stage from the homepage
3. Browse and flip method cards for detailed guidance
4. Click "Bank" to save important cards to your strategy
5. Use the journey map to view your saved cards
6. Export your final strategy as a text file

## Stages Overview

- **Stage A**: Design & Thesis Development
- **Stage B**: Strategy & Target Portfolio  
- **Stage C**: Awareness & Engagement
- **Stage D**: Financial & Non-Financial Support
- **Stage E**: Engagement & Application
- **Stage F**: Selection & Rejection
- **Stage G**: Portfolio Management
